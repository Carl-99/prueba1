Who are we?

We are a group of students from the Liceo Salvadoreño, we have the mission of improving reading habits within this institution and in the country, so we hope that we can create a useful page for this country and for the whole world.

Get to know the institution

We count with this advantages:

Our availability

As a large team, we have support during all business hours from Monday to Friday.

Book preservation

By using a web page, we can keep copies of books in the cloud without worrying that they will be lost beyond recovery.

Easy to share

By being a web page, it is easier to publicize the books than if they were physical and only found in Salvadoran libraries.

Accesible prices

All profits will go to the authors of their respective books, for which excessive prices will not be charged so that they can be known by as many people as possible.
